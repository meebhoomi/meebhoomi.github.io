MeeBhoomi (మీ భూమి, literally "Your Land") is the official online land records portal launched by the Revenue Department of the Government of Andhra Pradesh (AP), India.It is a major e-governance initiative aimed at digitizing all land records and providing citizens with easy, transparent, and direct access to their property information.
Key Services and Records Available on MeeBhoomi 
The portal, accessible at meebhoomi.ap.gov.in, allows the public to view and download various crucial land documents, typically searchable using District, Mandal, Village, and either the Khata Number (Account Number), Survey Number, or Aadhaar Number.Land Record DocumentPurpose and DetailsAdangal (అడంగల్) / Pahani Village 
Land Register: This is the most comprehensive document, providing details on land utilization, crops grown, owner's name, extent of land, type of soil, and cultivation history for a specific Survey Number.ROR-1B (Record of Rights)
Land Ownership Record: This document shows the name of the land owner (Pattadar), the nature of land possession, liabilities, and all recorded rights and interests in the land against a specific Khata Number.Village Map (గ్రామ పటం)Geographical Layout: Provides the map of the village, including the boundaries of different survey numbers, helping to visually identify the land parcel.
Electronic Passbook (E-Passbook)Digital Title Deed: A digital copy of the land title passbook issued to the landowner, which contains all the important land details.Aadhaar Linking StatusAllows citizens to check if their Aadhaar number is successfully linked with their land records for greater security and authentication.
Application StatusTracks the progress of various land-related applications, such as for Mutation (change of ownership details) or land conversion.How to Access RecordsVisit the official MeeBhoomi portal: meebhoomi.ap.gov.inOn the homepage, select the relevant option under the section for "Click for your land details" (మీ భూమి వివరాలకోసం క్లిక్ చేయండి), such as "Your Adangal/Village Adangal" or "Me 1-B/Village 1-B".
Fill in the required location details (District, Mandal, Village).Choose the search criterion (Khata Number, Survey Number, or Aadhaar Number) and enter the corresponding detail.Enter the CAPTCHA code and click submit to view the record.
